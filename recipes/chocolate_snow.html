Chocolate Snow

The Greatest Thing since Yellow Snow

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 0.25 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon powdered instant coffee
  • 1 gallon clean fresh snow

    Steps:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together milk, confectioners sugar, vanilla extract, cocoa powder, and instant coffee until the sugar has dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  2. Place snow in a large bowl, and pour the chocolate mixture over the snow. With a large spoon, stir until the ingredients are thoroughly combined. Serve immediately.
